The three card reading is one of the simplest tarot card spreads.


The left card represents events in your past that may have led to the position you are in now.


The center card represents your outlook on your current state of affairs.


The right card suggests a possible outcome if no action is taken to change the current course.


The future is not set in stone & can be changed through action.
